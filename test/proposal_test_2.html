CONTRACT ESSENTIALS

When the event specifications within the proposal are agreed upon, your Sales Manager will issue an agreement, often referred to as the contract. The following are key components:
  • Deposit - Required with returned signed contract/ license agreement
  • Insurance - A Certificate of Insurance naming the Peoria Civic Center and SMG as additional insured is required prior to move-in.   In addition, Waivers of Subrogation are required.
  • Ancillary Charges - rigging, electrical, equipment, AV, dedicated internet are some additional costs. Your Event Manager will work with you to provide cost estimates based on your event needs.
  • Security - Licensee is responsible for complete security within all contracted areas, from the beginning of the lease period until final move-out.
  • Catered Event Requirements- Show management shall provide to SavorPeoria the menu, estimated guest count and 50% deposit for the Food & Beverage service no later than ten (10) days prior to the contract start date.
