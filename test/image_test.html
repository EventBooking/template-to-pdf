  • Deposit - Required with returned signed contract/ license agreement

  • Ancillary Charges - rigging, electrical, equipment, AV, dedicated internet are some additional costs. Your Event Manager will work with you to provide cost estimates based on your event needs.
  • Security - Licensee is responsible for complete security within all contracted areas, from the beginning of the lease period until final move-out.
